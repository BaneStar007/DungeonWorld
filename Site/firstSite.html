Dungeon World
Dungeon World

Dungeon World online, is a world of players, interacting between each other in a fantasy world. Players play in five areas of the game.
Play as a Hero: Trek across lands, finding quests, seeking treasures, attaining legendary fame.
Own a building: Within established towns, or in bare wilderness, players may set up thier own buildings, either as housing for thier hero.. a Shop, a production workshop or the town hall as Mayor.
Build a City: Establish a town and entice local peasents and wild folk to settle within your town. establish trade routes between your town and other towns, to build your town into a city, a metropolis or an Empire.
Forge an Army: At some point empires will have disputes.. the common resoultion is war. Attacking your opponents, defending your towns .. establishing allies and enemies is important in the quest for empirical growth.
Pray to your god: Heros live as long as thier legends, Army commanders as long as war, towns and thier shops last many years.. but only the Gods themselves are immortal. Praying to a god can enhance your game, but the greatest power is from being the Avatar for your own personal god.. do you have what it takes?
Dungeon World RPG
Dungeon World RPG

Dungeon World the RPG is a Realistic Traditional Fantasy Roleplay Game, and by that we mean that The World that the game is set in and the rules by which the game is played, represent Realism at its core,
   E.G. The game rules follow the same rules of Science on what we can do as a person, if its possible for a regular human to do, its also possible for the character in the game, and what a regular human cannot do.. and regular character cannot also. but that is where it stops being Realistic.
   Like Any Traditional Roleplaying Game, Players wish to be the Adventurer,.. to step out of the 'world' into something more Fantastic, unreal and do things that normal humans are unable to do.
   Note: If you have only played computer Roleplaying Games before.. you may not realise that Roleplaying games existed before computer versions. The distinction is important, Computer Games cannot achieve even 1/10th what a Roleplaying game can. and this site cannot even come to 1/9th. the discussion of RPG vs CRPG takes place within the forums.

Escape From Reality
Escape From Reality

Escape From Reality is the Business title encompassing Dungeon World and other R.P.G's
  EFR Games has been in business since 1993, to produce Games titles and concepts for the broader RPG Market. The earliest Game was Dungeon World, brought into creation in 1993, and then Later expansions included DungeonWorld: Diabolis and DungeonWorld: Portals. A Realistic yet Fantasy based genre incorporating the Best of all RPG systems
  Around 1996, Travalaxy was spawned from the futuristic concepts, Space Opera and SPace Fantasy, More of a Trading game than anything else.. it started on a whole new path of RPG concepts including the packaged class & skills system.
   As a Fore-runner and as a Simple yet Fun game, 2015: Apocolypse was created, a world gone wrong, players hopes for survival depend on thier quick wits, and pure luck. The games quick character generation allowed for the difficult survival rate
   Dungeon Worlders: Players, was then created as a 'quick and easy' system designed for non-roleplayers to learn the basic concepts of Roleplaying as a game and as a concept itself.
