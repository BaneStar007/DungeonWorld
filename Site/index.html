Roleplaying Demo Team

Are you a Game Master? Are you a fan of games like Ars Magica, Dragon Warriors, Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay, and ..insert game..? Would you be excited to try a game built from the ground up using those ideals as an engine?

Then we have a mission for you.

Fill out and submit our Application to sign up. As a member of E.F.R.G.M.S you’ll gain exclusive access to scenarios, early release material, playtests, events, and more. Plus, every mission you complete as a member of the Trial GMS – running demo games, helping with community outreach, supporting new players – will earn you DW Gold, which you’ll eventually be able to use at the Coyn World Black Market.

Those interested in joining must be:

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Friendly, enthusiastic, patient, and outgoing personality
  • Able to read and follow written directions
  • Able to submit written reports and feedback
  • Able to communicate with E.F.R.Games HQ via the internet
  • Able to cover your own budget (including but not limited to: materials, lodging, meals, transportation, etc.)
  • And a burning desire to share games with others!

EFRGM are an all-volunteer corps. If you’re ready to take the leap, sign up NOW!

If you have questions, contact Escape From Reality Games at efrgms@dungeondelverstwelve.com.
