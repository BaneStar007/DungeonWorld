Dwarven Clocks in the Coyn World have 10 hours per day, known as shifts, which are almost 2.4 hours in Earth time, most dwarves rise at 2 and are in bed by 8, with breakfast at 3, lunch at 5 and dinner at 7, all very clean, clear and methodical, The Next smaller value are Periods, at approximately 14.4 Earth minutes, Many dwarves take a break at the 5th period of a shift and on the end of the shift, The minutes or minutia are also in 10, equating approximately to 86.4 Earth seconds, and a Dwarven pause, or second, takes 8 earth seconds.. which is why the phrase "give me a second" means a more realistic term for a dwarf than a human.
